About Us.

OI HUB is a student activity. Helps to discover the potentials buried inside students, support and improve them, in order to develop projects that help them get connected to the job market by:

  • Providing Courses and Camps for students.
  • Taking apart in global and local competition.
  • Organizing events to publish experience and culture of job market.
